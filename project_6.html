Shapes, Forms, Composition & Color (Adobe Illustrator) Project

For this project, I had to generate a new series of “subjective” graphic Icons. I had to create six different shapes with the use of the Pen Tool, pathfinder and the shape builder tool. After having all my six shapes, it was time to combine them with all the other shapes. In one of the graphic Icons, I used six different colors variation as a way to have this pattern-like color. Each of them had their own multi-color variation with a different background color. After having different graphic Icons, my final assignment was to create a digital Illustration that combines a series of my graphic Icons forms together. My favorite part of this project was to combine them into a Colossus.
